About Us

At Trust, we believe that the effective interplay between design and functionality is what sets our websites, web shops and apps apart from other digital agencies. We’re transparent with our customers and because we consider their projects to be our own, the results are beautiful, useful and responsive.

Rooted in a European work ethic and proudly established in Dubai, United Arab Emirates, all of our work is done from our gorgeous studio in the heart of Business Bay. This allows us greater control over our output, as well as the ability to create a true family atmosphere with our staff and clients. Our door is always open for a good cup of coffee and conversation.

How We Work

Our team is proficient in an array of services that can help elevate and empower your company or organization. We work with you to build comprehensive, thoughtful, and purpose-driven identities and experiences. Let’s talk about how our services can add value to your company.

01

Getting on the same page

Maybe you know exactly what you need — or maybe you have no idea what you want. Don't worry; we've developed a unique, step-by-step questionaire that will not only bring a clear picture of your desired website into focus, it will also help us determine the site's unique features (not to mention the exact cost). 

02

Magic in Motion

This is the fun part. Once we have a vision of where to take your website, we will put together a concept that not only looks great, but it will also translate your hopes for the site into reality. After you've had a chance to share your feedback with us, we'll then merge your suggestions into a final design.

03

Responsive Design

We believe one site fits all (screens). Or at least it should. This is called Responsive Design, which Trust builds into every project, allowing all of its websites to be accessible and user-friendly (for you and your customers) on pretty much any device, platform or browser. 

04

Behind the Scenes

The final design of your website will be brought to life using Concrete5 or Magento E-commerce, two user-friendly and insightful content management systems (CMS) that will allow you to get the most out of your website or webshop. 

05

On the Launch Pad

When it is time to go live, not only will we "flip the switch," but we'll register your site with Google Webmaster Tools (meaning that it will be searchable the next day) and set up Google Analytics so that you'll have an immediate idea of your visitor's behavior on your site. 

06

Lights, Camera, Action

Once the structure of your website is ready, you'll need to fill it with your content. If this step has always been intimidating, then you'll love our customized video manual, which walks you through every aspect of your website, pointing out helpful hints on uploading text and pictures, as well as customising menus and navigation. 

07

Maintaining your Edge

Trust* makes it easy for you to continually update your site while running your business. When you select our maintenance package, we'll take care of cropping and fine-tuning your photos, as well as integrating your content onto the site.
