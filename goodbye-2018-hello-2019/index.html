Goodbye 2018, Hello 2019!

Jan 02, 2019

by Patrick

Trust projects recap of the year. 2018 has been an amazing journey with inspiring clients to work for, a great team to work with, all within a brand new studio at the heart of Dubai. 2019, we're ready for you!

What a year it has been

There has been no sitting still in 2018. We've been working on amazing projects for amazing clients. We've expanded our team in development and in design. We're proud to now have a full app development department for both iOS and Android native apps. We've moved to a new studio in Business Bay at the heart of dubai. Our new work place with lots of day light, 3 meeting rooms, coffee corner and last but not least a balcony with spectacular views from the 34th floor of the PRISM tower. 2018 has shown us there is an enormous amount of potential in the Dubai market for the type of services and quality we have to offer. 2019, we're ready for you!
