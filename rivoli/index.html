The Brief

An elegant digital experience

Having a product portfolio of high-end brands the Rivoli Group website needs to look and breath the part. The simplistic interface, mainly composed out of a dark grey tone combined with white and olive green touches support this experience. Aiming for a simplistic yet elegant website we've embedded hover effects and transitions between pages and navigation.

Save

Navigation

Loads of pages, One solution

Given the activities and services of the Rivoli Group the website contains a variety of differently categorized pages. A hamburger menu based full screen navigation was the solution to allow the visitor of the site to navigate easily between pages. The fold-out effect with the olive green, only showing in transition, gives an extra touch.

Hover effects

Exploding heading and secondary brand colors appear on hover of the homepage blocks. Supporting the brand identity.
