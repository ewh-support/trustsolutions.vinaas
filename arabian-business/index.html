The Story

A heritage of business news

Part of the ITP Media group, Arabian Business is an institution among the thriving business community in Dubai. The key goal for the new site was to keep the existing users while growing the readership and advertising revenue. The success of the new design achieved an all-time high for the website. 

Features

Modular design allows editors to quickly compile organised reading lists that will keep users engaged.

Reader’s comments

User comments and engagement was a key feature with articles. Users can now see which stories are being discussed the most. ​​​​​​​

Gallery

A dedicated gallery section was another key traffic driver. It was important to integrate the same design UI within the gallery pages to match the article galleries.

Responsive

A first

A first for the website, the new site launched with a complete responsive design. Meaning the design can be viewed on all browser sizes, devices and mobiles with no compromise to the amount of information displayed or functions available.
