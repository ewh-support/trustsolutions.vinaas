Downtown Design

Middle East's Leading Design Fair

Downtown Design is the leading platform in the Middle East for the region’s trade professionals to discover original, high-quality design from all over the world. In its sixth edition, the fair will present a carefully edited roster of brands across a range of product categories; including furniture, lighting, bathrooms, kitchens, textiles and accessories.

The New Design

Global changes

The style just turned 5 years old in 2018 and commissioned to develop their new visual language. After exploring and analizing the publication’s graphic and editorial evolution throughout the years, we created a new modern identity to help Downtown Design have a more expressive and strong presence, cultural and contemporary art contexts. The new style has a classic inspiration, but with contemporary features, and was designed to have a solid and strong presence, keeping its sensibility and sophistication.
